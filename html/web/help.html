FEATURES


This application allows you to view all of the digital badges that you have earned in one easy to use platform

  • Ability to view all public badges placed in your personal groups
  • Search and filter badges many different ways including using: names, tags, and dates
  • Ability to add the badge to your Linkedin, Twitter, and Facebook profiles via the corresponding share Buttons


Requirements

  • To be able to use Western Badges you must have an account for a Mozilla Backpack using your wetsern email.
  • You must also have created groups in your backpack to be able to have them show up in this application
  • To be able to send the badge to your Linkedin profile you must have a Linkedin account
  • To be able to share the badge to your Twitter profile you must have a Twitter account
  • To be able to share the badge to your Facebook profile you must have a Facebook account


Tips

  • Try creating groups based off of similar features of the badges or by the courses that you were awarded them in
  • You must remember to make the badges public in the groups as well or else they will not be accessed
  • When sharing your badges on your Linkedin, Twitter, and Facebook profiles, make sure the badge is one that you really want to show off
