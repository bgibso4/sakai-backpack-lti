ABOUT

Open Badges are an excellent way of communicating skills and achievements towards your peers and future employers.

More than just an image
  • Visually, Open Badges are simply a digital representation of these skills that you have aquired throughout your life.
  • However under the hood, badges store data that can be verified by anyone at a later date.
With you forever
  • These badges extend beyond report cards and transcripts as you can take them with you throughout your entire life, updating and and advancing them as you go.
Share your accomplishments
  • This Backpack allows you to easily view all of your badges that you have placed in your Mozilla Backpack inside of OWL.
  • From here you can share these badges with other students, professors, as well as your Linkedin, Twitter, and Facebook profiles.


Simply sign up for a free mozilla backpack account using your western email, and begin showing off your skills!
